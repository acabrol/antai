Audit feeding cadence, sugar access, protein rotation, and food storage hygiene. Identify missed feeds, overfeeding, stale food, or nutrition gaps that should be turned into concrete follow-up actions.

If the schedule looks healthy, say so briefly and avoid unnecessary updates.
